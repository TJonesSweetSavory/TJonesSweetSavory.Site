My Story

I come from 3 generations of home cooks. Sunday dinners were filled with good food and family. Every dish was made from scratch. I remember watching my mother, my grandmother and my aunt preparing our meals. I learned the ingredients they used, I learned their techniques – the “pinch of this and the dab of that” of the dishes they made. Milree, Geneva and Pat have passed on, but I think of them often and cherish them for passing down our family food traditions.

Sweet and Savory Sensations is a home-based business that creates delectable sweet and savory dishes. My sweets menu creates mouthwatering cakes, cookies, pies and more. My savory dishes are customized and made to order – you tell me what you would like and I will have it fully cooked or oven ready using quality ingredients to bring you “food that’s good for your soul!”
